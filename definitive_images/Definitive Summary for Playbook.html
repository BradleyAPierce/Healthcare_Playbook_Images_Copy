What is Definitive Healthcare?  The Definitive Healthcare platform combines data, analytics and expertise to create healthcare commercial intelligence. It’s a healthcare database that provides insight on hospitals, clinics, long-term care facilities, imaging centers and surgery centers.  In addition to basic demographic information, it also includes the following data:

•       Facilities affiliations – health system-owned hospitals, hospital-owned clinics, extended care facilities, etc

•       GPO affiliations

•       Technology landscape – EHRs, RCM, Imaging, ECM, IT, Health Information Exchange, etc

•       CMS reported financial data

•       Referral volumes

•       Contact information to include virtually every key MGR, DIR, VP and C-suite role, administrative & clinical

•       Industry and facility news

Eventually we anticipate having integration with Salesforce, which will provide access to everyone in the sales organization.  Until that time, please reach out to your local, regional or national healthcare rep and they can assist in running reports.
